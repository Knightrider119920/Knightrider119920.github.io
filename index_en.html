About us

We are young company that focuses on distribution and production of mechanical spare parts for transport and industial technology. The name of our company comes from definion of latin word „fidelis", meaning reliable, which expresses one of the main principles of our operation. Within the portfolio of services we offer, we always try to provide our client with the most comprehensive solution to a specific demand.
